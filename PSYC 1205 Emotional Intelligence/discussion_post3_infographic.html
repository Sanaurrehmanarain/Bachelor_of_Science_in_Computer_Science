How to Maintain Your Emotions

Self-Awareness Check-ins

Regular moments throughout the day to assess emotional states and recognize patterns before they escalate.

Emotional Labeling

Specifically name emotions rather than using general terms. "I feel frustrated" vs "I feel bad."

Cognitive Reframing

Challenge automatic thoughts. Transform "This will be a disaster" into "This is an opportunity to learn."

Stress Management & Resilience Building

STOP Technique

Stop, Take a breath, Observe thoughts and feelings, Proceed mindfully.

4-7-8 Breathing

Inhale for 4 counts, hold for 7, exhale for 8. Activates the parasympathetic nervous system for immediate calm.

Meaning-Making

Find purpose in experiences, including difficult ones. Identify growth opportunities and personal values.

Social Connection

Build strong support networks that buffer against stress and offer different perspectives during challenges.

Emotional Balance & Impulse Control

The Pause Principle

Create space between trigger and response. Count to ten, take deep breaths, or step away temporarily.

Values-Based Decision Making

Ask "What response aligns with my core values?" rather than acting on immediate emotional impulses.

Emotional Surfing

Ride out intense emotions without immediately acting. Like waves, emotions naturally rise and fall.

Boundary Setting

Protect emotional resources by setting clear limits on energy expenditure and saying no when necessary.
